Privacy Policy for Crocy Tons

Last updated: February 2025

At Furniture Center Tech, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled in the game Crocy Tons.

1. No Data Collection

Crocy Tons is designed as a completely offline game. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, device data, or gameplay statistics from our users.

2. Offline Functionality

The game operates entirely on your local device. It does not require an internet connection to function and does not connect to any external servers or third-party services.

3. Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any personal data, our game is safe for children of all ages. We do not knowingly or unknowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect any user data, we cannot notify you of changes directly. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through our main website.
